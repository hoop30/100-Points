Rules

  • Create a new game to start playing, it will choose the first player to start

  • To play your turn you can roll the dice how many times you want. But if the result is 'one' you lose your score

  • Record your score by pressing the Hold button. It will keep your score safe and stop your turn.

  • Winner of the game is the first player to have 100 points !!
